## About Section

### About Blurb (2-3 lines)
```
I build software that solves real problems. With a focus on clean code, 
thoughtful design, and user experience, I create products that make a difference.

I'm currently [your current role/status]. Previously, I've worked on [brief mention 
of experience or notable projects].
```

*Alternative options:*

**Option 1 (Technical Focus):**
```
I'm a software engineer specializing in [your stack/domain]. I build scalable 
applications and systems that power modern businesses.

Currently working at [Company], where I focus on [key area]. I've shipped products 
used by [number/description] users.
```

**Option 2 (Product Focus):**
```
I design and build digital products that people love to use. My work spans 
from concept to launch, with a focus on solving meaningful problems.

I've helped companies [achievement/impact]. I'm passionate about [your passion] 
and believe in [your philosophy].
```

**Option 3 (Minimal):**
```
I build software. I solve problems. I ship products.

Currently [your role]. Previously [brief background]. Always learning.
```

## Tone Guidelines

- **Professional but approachable**
- **Concise and clear**
- **No jargon or buzzwords**
- **Direct and honest**
- **Helpful error messages**
- **Confident but not boastful**

## Usage Notes

1. Replace `[Your Name]` with your actual name
2. Replace `[your current role/status]` with your actual role
3. Replace `[brief mention of experience]` with your actual experience
4. Customize tagline to match your expertise
5. Adjust about blurb length based on your preference (2-3 lines recommended)
